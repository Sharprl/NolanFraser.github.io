Cybersecurity Risk Assessment

A business-focused assessment that ranks likely security risks, identifies practical controls, and presents recommendations for leadership review.

Project Overview

This project demonstrates how I evaluate cybersecurity risks by comparing likelihood, impact, affected assets, and business priority. The final output is a ranked remediation plan designed to help teams decide what to fix first.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Risk identification and prioritization
  • Likelihood and impact scoring
  • Control mapping and remediation planning
  • Security reporting for non-technical readers
